Works on key-value pairs.

Steps:
	1. Map input.
	2. Sort output from Map by key.
	3. Reduce sorted output from Map.

Example:
	1. Extract year and temperature from historical data.
	2. Sort by year.
	3. Find maximum temperature for each year.
